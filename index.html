About Us

Established in 2017, Jogimo Enterprises and General Construction Limited is a construction and infrastructural development company that undertakes a variety of projects including building,
water infrastructure, roads, and civil works among others.

We bring together a plethora of professionals with requisite capacity, capability and outstanding track records in undertaking and
managing major engineering projects.

We are currently operating in Kenya with prospect for greater Eastern Africa region. The company has successfully undertaken major projects across Kenya
in the most diverse conditions and terrains over the last 3 years. We take particular interest in the rehabilitation, refurbishment and maintenance of properties that are in our line of specialization
as stated above and are always prepared for the said in all aspects.
